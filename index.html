cat /about/bsides_coimbatore.txt

BSides Coimbatore is a local chapter in the global BSides network - a decentralized community of security researchers, hackers, and InfoSec professionals. We archive conference talks, host CTF competitions, coordinate hackathons, and facilitate knowledge exchange in the Coimbatore region.

Like InfoCon.org's mission to preserve hacking culture & DEFCON's spirit of open collaboration, we believe in freely sharing security knowledge, challenging conventional thinking, and building a resilient community. Our archive includes talks, workshops, CTF challenges, and research papers - all accessible to anyone curious about security.
